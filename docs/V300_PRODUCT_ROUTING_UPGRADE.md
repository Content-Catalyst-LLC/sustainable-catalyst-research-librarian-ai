# Research Librarian v3.0.0 Product Upgrade

The Research Librarian now serves as the routing layer across Sustainable Catalyst.

## Product role

- Research Librarian routes users.
- Workbench calculates, graphs, and inspects formulas.
- Decision Studio synthesizes Decision Packets and briefs.
- Modules produce artifacts.
- The Knowledge Library provides topic context.

## New capabilities

- Route map endpoint
- Health endpoint
- Exportable route notes
- Landing shortcode
- Route-map shortcode
- Gemini/OpenAI/disabled provider setting
- Deterministic fallback route system
- Stronger professional-boundary handling

## Suggested public placement

Use the full shortcode on the Research Librarian page:

```text
[sc_research_librarian title="Sustainable Catalyst Research Librarian"]
```

Use the landing mode on platform pages:

```text
[sc_research_librarian mode="landing" title="Sustainable Catalyst Research Librarian"]
```

