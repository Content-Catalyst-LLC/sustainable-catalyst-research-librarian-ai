# v5.7.0 — Research Demand and Knowledge-Gap Intelligence

## Purpose

Turn privacy-conscious Research Librarian operational signals into aggregate evidence about what visitors need, where routing confidence is weak, and where the Sustainable Catalyst knowledge system lacks sources, tools, or article-map coverage.

## Evidence streams

- Saved route sessions and handoff targets
- Low-confidence outcomes and zero-source sessions
- Visitor feedback and correction records
- Feature Suggestions contextual feedback bridge records
- Guided research path demand
- Retrieval evaluation failures

## Outputs

- 30-day, 90-day, and all-time windows
- High-demand routes and topic clusters
- Low-confidence route clusters
- Missing-source and missing-tool clusters
- Source-coverage summaries
- Advisory opportunity scores and recommended next actions
- Aggregate Site Intelligence events

## Privacy and governance

Public summaries are disabled by default and suppress clusters below a configurable minimum. Raw questions, conversation transcripts, email addresses, IP addresses, and API keys are not included in public output or shared platform events. Scores are advisory and require human review.
