# v6.0.0 — Integrated Research Guidance Platform

Research Librarian v6.0.0 consolidates the complete 5.x integration arc into a stable, governed research-guidance platform.

## Integrated workflow

1. Ask a site-scoped research question.
2. Route it through the Sustainable Catalyst knowledge architecture.
3. Review ranked source cards, confidence, and coverage.
4. Continue through an article-map or guided research path.
5. Send typed context to Workbench or Decision Studio.
6. Submit contextual ratings, corrections, gaps, or feature requests.
7. Aggregate research demand and knowledge-gap signals.
8. Apply only human-approved, regression-tested route improvements.

## Major additions

- Integrated platform command center.
- Unified module and capability status.
- Public guidance-platform and journey shortcodes.
- Administrator platform status and export endpoints.
- Versioned `sc-integrated-research-guidance/1.0` and `sc-research-guidance-journey/1.0` schemas.
- Privacy-minimized `librarian.guidance_platform_checked` events.
- Stable governance boundaries across feedback, AI, demand intelligence, and route improvement.

## Governance boundaries

The platform is site-scoped. AI outputs, visitor feedback, demand signals, and calculated opportunities remain advisory. Route changes require human approval and regression checks. Shared events do not contain raw conversations, API keys, IP addresses, or required personal data.
