# Research Librarian AI v7.0.0

## Connected Research Intelligence Platform

v7.0.0 consolidates the Research Librarian into a stable project environment. WordPress remains the publishing, administration, identity, and recovery boundary. FastAPI provides durable project state, verified retrieval, workflow orchestration, portable backups, and typed cross-product contracts.

## Persistent research model

The platform stores private-by-default research projects, multi-step investigations, evidence entities, annotations, reading paths, contradiction reports, uncertainty registers, reusable workflow templates, handoffs, receipts, and returned artifacts. Every entity receives a stable identifier, timestamp, and tamper-evident fingerprint.

## Generation boundary

Retrieval, storage, project state, evidence, governance, and deterministic fallback are provider-independent. Generated synthesis is accessed through an adapter boundary and may be replaced without rebuilding the durable project model.

## Backup and recovery

A project backup contains the project, investigations, entities, handoff references, and artifact history. Its canonical checksum is verified before dry-run inspection or import. Import does not publish content or invoke connected products.

## Human control

Project creation, evidence promotion, workflow execution, handoff delivery, artifact acceptance, source governance, release overrides, and publication remain explicit human actions.
